## 1. Entity, positioning & brand relationships (the framing that governs everything)
- **kairali.guru = the TRAINING / education entity** — a dedicated portal for Kairali's Ayurveda training programmes. It is **its own entity, distinct from the healing village.**
- **Positioning wedge:** *"The only Ayurveda training brand that is authentically Kerala AND accessible worldwide"* — 118-year lineage, a **real NABH-accredited hospital-village**, physicians teaching on **real patients**, online + in person.
- **Lineage:** family practice **since 1908** (confirmed on Kairali's own footer). "**Founded 1989**" = the modern Kairali Ayurvedic Centre. Keep the **1908** framing for lineage; 1989/25-yrs is the company.
- **The healing village (KTAHV)** = `ayurvedichealingvillage.com` = 65-acre NABH-accredited Ayurvedic hospital-retreat, Kodumbu, Palakkad, Kerala. It's a **separate site** (treatment-focused). "**Kairali Guru**" already exists there as a yoga/meditation/learning sub-brand.
- **Certification body:** **Kairali Institute of Panchakarma Therapies / Kairali Ayurvedic Centre Pvt Ltd.**

## 2. ⚠️ TWO LOCATIONS ONLY — Kerala + Delhi (a hard correction)

## 7. Compliance nuance (the key distinction that trips people up)
- **kairali.guru is the TRAINING entity → it is NOT under KTAHV's GST Show-Cause-Notice clinical-language lock.** So **"retreat / wellness / immersive / journey"** framing is **fine** here. (KTAHV's `ayurvedichealingvillage.com` is under the lock — clinical register only; banned there: spa, resort, holiday, retreat, luxury, cure, guest. Do NOT apply that lock to kairali.guru.)
- **But standard web compliance still applies** (markets: EU/GCC/Russia/NA): **GDPR/ePrivacy** (consent-gated analytics), **EAA/WCAG 2.2 AA** (+ accessibility statement, no overlay widgets), **EU AI Act** (chatbot AI-disclosure), **India DPDP**, **CCPA**.
