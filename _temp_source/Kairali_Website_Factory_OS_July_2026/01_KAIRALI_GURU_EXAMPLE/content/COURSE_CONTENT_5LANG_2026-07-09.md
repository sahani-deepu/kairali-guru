## 2. Each programme (English master — go-live)

**OAP-HV — One Day Ayurveda Workshop (1 day · 3 hrs · Beginner · ₹18,400 / $307).** A single-day introduction to Ayurveda with a certificate — its principles, the three doshas, and a first taste of practice. Ideal for groups, travellers, corporates, and clubs who want an authentic, guided first step.

**TAP-HV — Three-Day Short-Term Certificate (3 days · 9 hrs · Beginner · ₹55,200 / $921).** A short, structured certificate covering Ayurveda's foundations and introductory therapies. Suited to spa & wellness staff, salon managers and owners, IT professionals, and diplomats who want grounding without a long stay.

**WAP-HV — Intensive Ayurveda Training (5 days · 15 hrs · Intermediate · ₹92,000 / $1,535).** Intensive, hands-on training in the five core therapies — Abhyangam, Shirodhara, Podikizhi, Udwarthanam and Kativasti — alongside Ayurvedic philosophy and wellness concepts. For professionals ready to practise confidently.

**AATP1-HV — Level-One Advanced for Wellness Professionals (10 days · 40 hrs · Advanced · ₹184,000 / $3,070).** Kairali's most sought-after programme. Master the basic Ayurvedic therapies and learn to run basic wellness consultations — theory plus supervised practical (Abhyangam, Shirodhara, Nasya and more), living on campus for the ten days.

**AALCT-HV — Advanced Lifestyle Consultant & Therapist (20 days · 80 hrs · Advanced · ₹368,000 / $6,140).** The complete practitioner certification: the full service list, ample supervised practice, and mastery across all services — including Prakriti (constitution) analysis, management of lifestyle disorders, and up to nine major Ayurvedic therapies. For those building a career as a lifestyle consultant or therapist.

Certificates issued by the **Kairali Institute of Panchakarma Therapies / Kairali Ayurvedic Centre Pvt Ltd**. Theory by Ayurvedic Doctors; practicals by Senior Ayurvedic Trainers & Doctors. **To enrol:** valid photo ID + two passport-size photographs.

## 3. How the programme works (6 steps · English master)
1. **Orientation** — core concepts, history, philosophy, and the three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha).
2. **Theory** — Ayurvedic anatomy & physiology, herbal medicine and formulations, diet and lifestyle management.
3. **Practical training** — hands-on therapies (Abhyanga, Shirodhara, Panchakarma techniques) plus preparing herbal remedies and Ayurvedic dishes.
4. **Yoga & meditation** — integrated daily for mind-body harmony.
5. **Personalised feedback** — instructors refine your technique throughout.

**Features:** broad curriculum + expert faculty · practical training you can tailor (therapy / nutrition / management) · interactive, holistic learning · recognised certification + continuing support.
**Benefits:** knowledge from basics to advanced · practical skills + certification · calmer, more centred mind · a learning path customised to you.
