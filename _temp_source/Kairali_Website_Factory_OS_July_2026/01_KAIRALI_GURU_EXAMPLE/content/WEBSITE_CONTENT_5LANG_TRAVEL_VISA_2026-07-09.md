## TRAVEL, VISA & GETTING HERE

### EN
**Coming to study with us**
**Getting here.** Our Kerala healing village is in Palakkad — Kairali, P.O. Olassery, Kodumbu, Palakkad District 678551, Kerala. The nearest airport is Coimbatore (CJB), about 62 km / roughly an hour away; Kochi (COK) is about 115 km. By rail, Palakkad Junction (PGT) is about 18 km. We arrange airport and station transfers — just share your arrival details. Our Delhi centre offers the same programmes without on-campus accommodation; the nearest airport is Delhi (DEL). *(Delhi address to be added.)*
**Staying with us.** In Kerala, your stay on our green campus is included with residential programmes — you live where you learn, among the medicinal gardens, with daily meals prepared to Ayurvedic principles (vegetarian, and mindful of dietary needs). In Delhi, the programme is non-residential; we're happy to suggest places to stay nearby.
**Visa & entry.** Most international learners visit India on an **e-Visa**, applied for online before travel. Visa category and eligibility depend on your nationality and the length of your programme, and rules change from time to time — please confirm the current requirement on the official Indian e-Visa portal or with your nearest Indian embassy or consulate. We provide an enrolment letter and guidance to support your application.
**What to bring.** A valid photo ID (passport for international learners) and two passport-size photographs. We'll send joining details once your place is confirmed.
*Learners join us from around the world — our team will help you plan every step.*
