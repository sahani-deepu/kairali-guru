# PART A — Buyer journey by market

Design principle for the flow: **answer the biggest fear first, then build desire, then remove friction, then make the next step tiny.** Each market fears something different and buys differently — the flow adapts, the enrolment path stays one calm step (advisor call / WhatsApp).

### EN — International + North America + NRI diaspora (MOA: M3 diaspora + global)
- **Mindset:** "Is it authentic and will the certificate be worth it?" NRIs already respect Ayurveda and want the *real Kerala source*; Westerners benchmark against US schools (Vasant Lad). Outcome + credibility + flexibility.
- **Top questions:** Is it recognised / can I practise with it? How long, and is there an online option? Total cost and what's included? Is Kerala safe and comfortable? Food? How do I get there and do I need a visa? Where do I stay? Can I combine it with travel?
- **Buying pattern:** research-heavy, reads reviews, compares, wants clear ROI; books online but expects a fast, human follow-up. NRIs often decide quicker (family ties to India).
- **Flow we curate:** Home (authentic Kerala + 1908 + physician-led) → Learn article (earn trust) → Course page (outcome + certificate + duration + what's included) → Travel/visa/stay (reassurance) → Enquiry (a physician-advisor calls) → enrol.

### DE — DACH (MOA: M4a — via specialist agency *and* direct)
- **Mindset:** rigour and legitimacy. "Show me exactly what I get." Germans research deeply and reward precision, structure, and proof; hype repels them.
- **Top questions:** What precisely is the curriculum (hours, therapies)? Who teaches, with what qualifications? Is it accredited, and what certificate exactly? Cancellation/refund terms? The exact schedule (days, hours/day)? Accommodation standard + cost? Climate and health prep? Visa specifics? What's included, itemised?
- **Buying pattern:** methodical and thorough; wants everything documented before committing; values transparency and formal register (**Sie**); may come via a specialist agency after due diligence.
- **Flow we curate:** lead with the **detailed curriculum + credentials + itemised inclusions + transparent terms + precise logistics**, then a calm, factual CTA. Depth = trust here.

### FR — France (MOA: M4b — via specialist agency *and* direct)
- **Mindset:** elegance and authentic experience. The *journey* matters as much as the certificate — art-de-vivre, the sensory Kerala.
- **Top questions:** What is the experience like — the setting, the authenticity? What certificate, and how long? The accommodation and its ambiance? The food? How to get there, visa, cost?
- **Buying pattern:** decides on emotional resonance + credibility + refinement; wants an elegant, reassuring flow; formal register (**vous**).
- **Flow we curate:** open with the **authentic Kerala experience + lineage** (beautifully presented), then credibility, then logistics — refined throughout.

### AR — GCC (MOA: M6 — now in scope for this portal)
- **Mindset:** luxury, **privacy and discretion**, family, prestige, comfort, trust. Cultural and dietary sensitivity matter.
- **Top questions:** Is it private and discreet? Is the accommodation comfortable? Is the food halal/vegetarian? Is it family-appropriate — can family join? Direct flights, and is the visa easy for GCC nationals? Cost and premium options? Is it exclusive/prestigious? Will I be met at the airport?
- **Buying pattern:** values discretion, premium comfort, and relationships; prefers **personal handling over WhatsApp/phone**; decides on trust + comfort + status.
- **Flow we curate:** foreground **privacy, comfort, dietary care, family welcome, airport reception**; keep enrolment discreet and personal (**WhatsApp / advisor**), not a public form dump. Warm, unhurried, premium.

### RU — Russia + CIS (MOA: new for this portal; Yandex-led)
- **Mindset:** results, prestige, value, authenticity. Russians know and seek Ayurveda-India; they want the real thing *and* tangible outcomes.
- **Top questions:** What results/skills will I actually gain? Is it authentic? What is the certificate worth? Duration and cost — is it good value? Accommodation? Flights and visa? Is there Russian-language support?
- **Buying pattern:** results- and value-driven; researches and reads reviews (via Yandex); wants concrete, engaging info; decides on outcomes + trust.
- **Flow we curate:** lead with **authentic lineage + concrete outcomes + value + Russian-language support + trip support**; keep the RU experience fast and genuinely native (Yandex rewards engagement).
