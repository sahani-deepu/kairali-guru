## Content guidance for the AI writer
The AI building Kairali Guru content should treat these keywords as semantic themes, not as isolated phrases to repeat mechanically.[cite:12][cite:17][cite:18] Each major page should have one primary keyword, three to five secondary keywords, and a supporting set of question-based phrases that can be answered in short paragraphs and FAQ blocks.[cite:17][cite:18]

Recommended usage pattern:

- Home page: focus on branded, broad, and trust-building keywords such as "kairali guru ayurveda training," "ayurveda certification course," and "kerala ayurveda training."[cite:13][cite:20][cite:22]
- Course category pages: target terms such as "ayurveda online course," "panchakarma training," "ayurvedic nutrition course," and "ayurveda wellness course."[cite:10][cite:19][cite:20]
- Individual course pages: optimize around exact course intent such as "panchakarma technician course" or "beginner ayurveda course," supported by outcome-focused phrases and FAQs.[cite:11][cite:12][cite:22]
- Blog and learning hub pages: answer question-led AEO searches such as "how to learn ayurveda" and "best ayurveda course for beginners."[cite:12][cite:17]
- FAQ sections: use conversational phrasing and concise answers to improve visibility in AI-generated results and featured answer surfaces.[cite:17][cite:18]
